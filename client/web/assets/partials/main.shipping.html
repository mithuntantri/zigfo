Shipping Policy
WE OFFER FREE SHIPPING ANYWHERE IN INDIA Please allow 2-3 weeks for your order to be Design, Manufacture and dispatched and it will take about 1-2 additional week for it to reach you depending on your proximity to our dispatch location. Normally for orders placed before 12:00 PM on a business day, we aim to start processing it the same day, provided that the card security checks are complete, payment is received and the stock availability is confirmed. Orders placed after 12:00 PM will be start process-flow the next business day. Orders Stitched on Sunday or during Holidays are dispatched the following Monday or the next working day. During busy times, such as holiday periods, there can be processing and shipping delays. The orders cannot be shipped to PO boxes or military addresses, and rural domestic addresses require one or more additional days to deliver. Orders requiring engraving or any customization will require additional time and will be shipped in additional 3 -5 working days. If in any case you receive a package which is damaged during transit then please do not accept it. You can return the same and contact us asking for another piece. Please follow the same in case you've received an incorrect product and we would appreciate it if you wouldn't open the packaging. We will try our level best to send you a replacement as soon as possible. In case of custom made products and garments we would re-stitch or re-size the product as per your request for free of cost for the one time.
WE OFFER INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING For international shipping it may take another 1-2 weeks depending upon the Currier Services and charges applicable accordingly.
